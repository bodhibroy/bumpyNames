Rules of the Game

  1. The arena has a lot of coins!
  2. Objective: try and collect as many coins in the quickest time possible.
  3. You will receive one point for every unique coin you collect.
  4. Use the arrow keys to move your avatar
    • ← (Left)
    • → (Right)
    • ↑ (Up) and
    • ↓ (Down)
  5. Try to be courteous to others. Or BLOCK THEM. WHATEVER.
